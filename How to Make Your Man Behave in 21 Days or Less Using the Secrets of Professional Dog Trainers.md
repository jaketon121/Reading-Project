# 半碗 - half-bowl 
要让一只狗保持讨好的状态，就每次给他半碗的满足感就好，然后他每一次就会想要更多。
（A known trick for keeping a dog on his best behavior is to just fill his bowl halfway so he's always yearning for more.）
